Alice and Willetta

(Yes, I made this about my cats. I'm sorry.)

Pictured: Alice (left) and Willetta (right)

Origin Story

Alice and Willetta were both feral barn kittens born somewhere in Multnomah county. Willetta was found attempting to break into someone's house during a thunderstorm. Alice led a peaceful life in someone's barn. Fate brought them together at a no-kill shelter where the unlikely duo bonded. They were adopted as a pair by a couple of weirdos in NE Portland.

Habits

Alice and Willetta both enjoy:

  1. running through the house
  2. chewing on silvervine sticks
  3. yowling loudly every night at the same time for treats

Compared to Willetta, Alice is shy and quiet. She can usually be found hiding under the couch. On the other hand, Willetta assumes all other living things are for her entertainment, and will aggressively remind such beings of this fact. Willetta's favorite activity is waking her humans up at 6 a.m. demanding attention.

Fun Facts

  • Alice has a clipped ear. We think this means she was caught and spayed and then released when she was a kitten.
  • Willetta has a digestive abnormality which makes it so she can only eat certain foods. Normal cat food causes terrifying problems that are difficult to clean up afterward ._.
  • Alice curls her back feet when she yawns (not really a fun fact, just adorable.)
  • Willetta was taken away from her mother too early as a kitten, and now she thinks her humans flesh are a good source of milk o_o
